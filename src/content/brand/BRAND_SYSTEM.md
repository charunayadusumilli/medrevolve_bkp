# MedRevolve · Brand System

> Reference document. Anyone making something for the brand reads this first.

---

## 01 — What MedRevolve is

A white-label B2B platform for telehealth and wellness businesses. Complete infrastructure issued
as modules — storefront, payments, fulfillment, telehealth, compliance, provider network — sold
to operators (wellness merchants, med spas, clinics, fitness coaches, MD-led practices),
not to end consumers.

**One-line positioning.** *Complete wellness commerce OS — white-label telehealth platform.*

**The headline tagline.** *Build. Sell. Comply. Under your brand.*

**The value proposition.** *Launch your telehealth or wellness business in days, not months.*

**The aesthetic posture.** Editorial. Considered. Adult. Not "wellness sparkle."
Not "supplement bro." Not a SaaS-purple gradient.

- **Primary colors:** Cream/off-white (#FDFBF7), Deep brown/black (#0A0A0A)

## 07 — Prohibited

- Unsanctioned health claims
- Patient testimonials (not B2B, not our customer)
- Hype language ("revolutionary," "disruption," "game-changing")
- Promises we can't keep compliance-wise
- Affiliate links or MLM-style promotion
- Consumer-facing language (we're B2B only)
- Misrepresenting our role (we're infrastructure, not a consumer brand)

## 09 — Brand Voice

✓ **Professional, not corporate** — We sound like founders, not marketing
✓ **Direct, not clever** — Say what we mean. No wordplay.
✓ **Transparent about compliance** — It's our differentiator, not a burden
✓ **Operator-first** — We talk to merchants, not about them
✓ **B2B only** — No consumer pitches. No patient language.
✓ **Data-backed** — Numbers, not adjectives. 200+ merchants, 7 days, 50 states.
