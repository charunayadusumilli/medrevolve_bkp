# MedRevolve · Brand System

> Reference document. Anyone making something for the brand reads this first.

---

## 01 — What MedRevolve is

A B2B platform for the peptide and GLP economy. Compliant infrastructure issued
as modules — storefront, payments, fulfillment, telehealth, compliance — sold
to operators (peptide vendors, fitness coaches, MD-led wellness practices),
not to end consumers.

**One-line positioning.** *Compliant infrastructure for the peptide and GLP
economy.*

**The headline tagline.** *Twenty founding operators. Twenty-five storefronts.*

**The aesthetic posture.** Editorial. Considered. Adult. Not "wellness sparkle."
Not "supplement bro." Not a SaaS-purple gradient.

## 03 — Visual Language

- **Primary colors:** Cream/off-white (#FDFBF7), Deep brown/black (#0A0A0A)
- **Accent colors:** Sage green (#4A6741), Warm brown (#8B6F47)
- **Typography:** Serif (headlines), sans-serif (body) — editorial, adult
- **Imagery:** Compliance cards, operator workspaces, clean product shots
- **Compliance ledger:** Always visible. Part of the brand. DK/flag icons for status.

---

## 04 — Key Messages

1. **Infrastructure, not consumer service** — We sell to operators, not patients
2. **Compliance built-in** — Not bolted on. Every module ships compliant.
3. **Modular, not monolithic** — Mix and match what you need
4. **Founding cohort posture** — Exclusive. Vetted. Early access.
5. **Two tracks:** GLP (consumer) and RUO (research-use-only)

---

## 05 — Content Pillars

- **Operator stories** — How founders use MedRevolve
- **Compliance deep-dives** — Documentary-style education
- **Product releases** — Module announcements, feature launches
- **Founder notes** — Transparent, educational, behind-the-scenes
- **Peptide University** — Educational content for the ecosystem

---

## 06 — Prohibited

- Unsanctioned health claims
- Patient testimonials (not B2B, not our customer)
- Hype language ("revolutionary," "disruption," etc.)
- Promises we can't keep compliance-wise
- Affiliate links or MLM-style promotion
